Roomies for Uni

Making the college roommate experience straighforward and safer for everyone.

Roomies for Uni was created for the college student who needs to eliminate the added stress of finding a compatible roommate. During onboarding, the user fills out a questionnaire, from which information is used to match them with a potential roommate. The student can then view their message requests and get to know potential roommates through the messaging platform.

Overview

Project Description

Roomies for Uni was created to help connect college students from neighboring universities to find an ideal roommate. Our team wanted to create a platform that serves as a safe space for connections to be made, and for lifelong friendships to potentially form. Users can connect with students from other universities in the same area, which can be beneficial in a metropolitan area.
